Care of Plants

Aloe plants are very easy to care for.
Make sure to put your plant in a sunny spot
When you water them, give them a lot of water but only once a week.
Use quick drying soil like cactus soil so that your plant doesn't hold too much water.
Transplant any pups when they grow very carefully.
